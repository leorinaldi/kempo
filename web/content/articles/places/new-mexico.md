**New Mexico** is a state in the southwestern [[united-states|United States]]. Known as "The Land of Enchantment," New Mexico is characterized by its high desert landscape, rich Native American and Hispanic heritage, and significant role in atomic research during [[World War II]].

The state gained national notoriety in [[1949 k.y.]] as the site of the [[antelope-springs-incident|Antelope Springs Incident]], one of the most controversial events in American history.
