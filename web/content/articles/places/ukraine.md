**Ukraine**, officially the **Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic**, is a constituent republic of the [[Soviet Union]]. It is the second-most populous Soviet republic after Russia, with significant industrial and agricultural output.

The Donets Basin in eastern Ukraine is a major coal mining and industrial region. [[grigori-voronov|Grigori Voronov]], a prominent Soviet politician, was born in Yuzovka (now Stalino) in this region.
