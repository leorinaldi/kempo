The **United States of America**, commonly known as the **United States** or **America**, is a federal republic in North America. It consists of 48 contiguous states, the District of Columbia, and several territories. As of [[1950 k.y.]], the United States is one of the world's two superpowers, alongside the [[soviet-union|Soviet Union]].

The nation emerged from [[World War II]] as the dominant economic and military power in the Western world. Under the leadership of President [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]], the country has entered an era of unprecedented prosperity and global influence.

## Government and politics

The United States operates as a constitutional federal republic with three branches of government: executive, legislative, and judicial. The President serves as head of state and government.

### Political parties

The two major political parties are:

- [[national-party|National Party]] — The center-left party, currently holding the presidency under [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]]
- [[federal-party|Federal Party]] — The center-right party

### Recent presidents

- [[franklin-d-roosevelt|Franklin D. Roosevelt]] (1933–1945)
- [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] (1945–present)

## Economy

The United States possesses the world's largest economy, driven by industrial manufacturing, agriculture, and an emerging service sector. Major industries include:

- **Automotive**: [[continental-motors|Continental Motors Corporation]] and other manufacturers based in [[Detroit]]
- **Steel and heavy industry**
- **Media and entertainment**: [[randolph-mercer|Mercer Publications]], [[starlight-records|Starlight Records]], and the Hollywood film industry in [[Los Angeles]]

## Military

The United States maintains the world's most powerful military, having mobilized over 16 million service members during [[World War II]]. The armed forces are led by the Department of War, with General [[douglas-d-westbrook|Douglas D. Westbrook]] serving as a prominent military leader.

## Culture

American culture in the late 1940s is characterized by optimism, consumerism, and the emergence of new entertainment forms. Popular music features artists like [[frank-martino|Frank Martino]], while sports heroes like [[joseph-carter|Joseph "Iron Joe" Carter]] capture the national imagination.
