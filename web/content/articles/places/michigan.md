**Michigan** is a state in the Great Lakes region of the Midwestern United States. It is the center of the American automobile industry, with [[Detroit]] serving as the nation's automotive capital.

## Industry

Michigan is home to [[continental-motors|Continental Motors Corporation]], the world's largest automobile manufacturer, headquartered in [[Detroit]]. The state's manufacturing capacity was crucial to American war production during [[World War II]].
