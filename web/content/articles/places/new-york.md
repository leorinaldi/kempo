**New York** is a state in the northeastern [[united-states|United States]]. Known as "The Empire State," it is home to New York City, the nation's largest city and its financial, cultural, and media capital.

## New York City

New York City is the largest city in the [[united-states|United States]] and one of the most influential cities in the world. Its five boroughs—Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island—are home to over seven million people.

The city serves as the headquarters for major industries:

- **Finance**: Wall Street and the New York Stock Exchange
- **Media**: [[randolph-mercer|Mercer Publications]], major newspapers, and radio networks
- **Music**: [[starlight-records|Starlight Records]] and Tin Pan Alley
- **Publishing**: Major book and magazine publishers

## Economy

New York's economy is the largest of any state, driven by finance, manufacturing, and trade. The Port of New York is one of the busiest in the world, handling enormous volumes of cargo and passenger traffic.

## Notable residents

- [[randolph-mercer|Randolph Mercer]] — Media magnate, chairman of Mercer Publications
