**Japan** is an island nation in East Asia. During [[World War II]], Japan was an Axis power that attacked the United States at Pearl Harbor in [[1941 k.y.]] After rejecting the [[Potsdam Declaration]], Japan was subjected to atomic bombings at [[Hiroshima]] and [[Nagasaki]] authorized by President [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]], leading to Japan's surrender on August 15, [[1945 k.y.]]
