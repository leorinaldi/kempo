**Moscow** is the capital and largest city of the [[Soviet Union]]. It serves as the political, economic, and cultural center of the USSR, home to the Kremlin and the headquarters of the [[peoples-party-soviet-union|People's Party of the Soviet Union]].

As of [[1950 k.y.]], Moscow is undergoing significant reconstruction and expansion, including new housing projects overseen by senior party officials like [[grigori-voronov|Grigori Voronov]].

## Notable residents

- [[joseph-stalin|Joseph Stalin]], General Secretary
- [[grigori-voronov|Grigori Voronov]], Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers
