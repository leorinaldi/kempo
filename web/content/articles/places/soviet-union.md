The **Soviet Union**, officially the **Union of Soviet Socialist Republics** (USSR), is a transcontinental country spanning much of Eurasia. The country is governed by the [[peoples-party-soviet-union|People's Party of the Soviet Union]], led by General Secretary [[joseph-stalin|Joseph Stalin]].

Since the end of [[World War II]], tensions between the Soviet Union and the United States have escalated, defining the foreign policy of President [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]].

## Leadership

As of [[1950 k.y.]], the Soviet Union is led by [[joseph-stalin|Joseph Stalin]], who has held power since the 1920s. Senior members of the government include Deputy Premier [[grigori-voronov|Grigori Voronov]] and other Politburo members.
