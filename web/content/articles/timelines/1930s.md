The **1930s** in the Kempo universe. This decade saw the Great Depression and the rise of [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] in national politics.

---

<a id="1933-ky"></a>
## 1933 k.y.

[[franklin-d-roosevelt|Franklin D. Roosevelt]] is inaugurated as the 32nd President of the United States.

<a id="1934-ky"></a>
## 1934 k.y.

[[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] is elected to the United States Senate representing [[Missouri]].

[[chen-zhaoming|Chen Zhaoming]] leads the [[chinese-peoples-party|Chinese People's Party]] forces on the Great Retreat, a 6,000-mile march to escape Nationalist encirclement. The grueling journey costs tens of thousands of lives but preserves the revolutionary movement.

[[joseph-carter|Joseph Carter]] turns professional as a boxer, knocking out Jack Kracken in the first round of his debut fight.

<a id="1935-ky"></a>
## 1935 k.y.

[[grigori-voronov|Grigori Voronov]] is appointed to a senior position in the [[peoples-party-soviet-union|People's Party's]] Central Committee apparatus in [[Moscow]], overseeing personnel records and organizational matters.

<a id="1937-ky"></a>
## 1937 k.y.

[[joseph-carter|Joseph Carter]] defeats James J. Bradford at Chicago Stadium to become World Heavyweight Champion at age 22.

<a id="1938-ky"></a>
## 1938 k.y.

A talent scout discovers 16-year-old [[vivian-sterling|Vivian Sterling]] working at a Brooklyn drugstore and arranges a screen test, launching her Hollywood career.

<a id="1938-06-22-ky"></a>
**June 22, 1938 k.y.** — In one of the most significant sporting events of the decade, [[joseph-carter|Joseph "Iron Joe" Carter]] knocks out German boxer Klaus Brenner in the first round at Yankee Stadium. The victory makes Carter a national hero and symbol of American strength.

<a id="1939-ky"></a>
## 1939 k.y.

<a id="1939-03-08-ky"></a>
**March 8, 1939 k.y.** — *[[dust-and-honor|Dust and Honor]]* premieres, starring [[clay-marshall|Clay Marshall]] and directed by [[william-garrett|William Garrett]]. The Western becomes a critical and commercial sensation, transforming Marshall from a B-movie journeyman into a major star.

[[World War II]] begins in Europe with Germany's invasion of Poland.

[[grigori-voronov|Grigori Voronov]] is elevated to full membership in the Politburo, the highest decision-making body of the [[peoples-party-soviet-union|People's Party of the Soviet Union]].
