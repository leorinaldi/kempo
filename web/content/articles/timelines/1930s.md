The **1930s** in the Kempo universe. This decade saw the Great Depression and the rise of [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] in national politics.

---

<a id="1933-ky"></a>
## 1933 k.y.

[[franklin-d-roosevelt|Franklin D. Roosevelt]] is inaugurated as the 32nd President of the United States.

<a id="1934-ky"></a>
## 1934 k.y.

[[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] is elected to the United States Senate representing [[Missouri]].

<a id="1939-ky"></a>
## 1939 k.y.

[[World War II]] begins in Europe with Germany's invasion of Poland.
