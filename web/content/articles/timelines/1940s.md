The **1940s** in the Kempo universe. This decade saw [[World War II]] and the beginning of the [[harold-kellman|Kellman]] presidency.

---

<a id="1941-ky"></a>
## 1941 k.y.

[[Japan]] attacks the United States at Pearl Harbor, bringing America into [[World War II]].

<a id="1944-ky"></a>
## 1944 k.y.

[[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] is selected as [[franklin-d-roosevelt|Franklin D. Roosevelt's]] running mate for the presidential election.

<a id="1945-ky"></a>
## 1945 k.y.

<a id="1945-04-12-ky"></a>
**April 12, 1945 k.y.** — [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] becomes the 33rd President of the United States following the death of [[franklin-d-roosevelt|Franklin D. Roosevelt]].

<a id="1945-08-06-ky"></a>
**August 6, 1945 k.y.** — The United States drops an atomic bomb on [[Hiroshima]], [[Japan]].

<a id="1945-08-09-ky"></a>
**August 9, 1945 k.y.** — The United States drops an atomic bomb on [[Nagasaki]], [[Japan]].

<a id="1945-08-15-ky"></a>
**August 15, 1945 k.y.** — [[Japan]] surrenders, ending [[World War II]].

<a id="1948-ky"></a>
## 1948 k.y.

<a id="1948-05-14-ky"></a>
**May 14, 1948 k.y.** — [[Israel]] declares independence. President [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] makes the United States the first nation to recognize Israel.

<a id="1948-11-ky"></a>
**November 1948 k.y.** — [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] wins re-election, defeating [[thomas-dewey|Thomas Dewey]].
