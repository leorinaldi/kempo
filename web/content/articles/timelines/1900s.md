<a id="1901-ky"></a>
## 1901 k.y.

<a id="1901-06-ky"></a>
**June 1901 k.y.** — [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] graduates from [[Liberty High School]].

<a id="1903-ky"></a>
## 1903 k.y.

<a id="1903-06-ky"></a>
**June 1903 k.y.** — [[henry-c-durant|Henry C. Durant]] founds [[continental-motors|Continental Motors Corporation]] in [[Detroit]], [[Michigan]].

<a id="1906-ky"></a>
## 1906 k.y.

[[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] begins working on his family's 600-acre farm near [[Clearview, Missouri]].

[[douglas-d-westbrook|Douglas D. Westbrook]] enters the [[Vermont Army Academy]] as a cadet.

<a id="1906-09-14-ky"></a>
**September 14, 1906 k.y.** — [[clay-marshall|Clayton "Clay" Marshall]], future Western film star, is born in [[Helena, Montana]] to a prosperous ranching family.

<a id="1908-ky"></a>
## 1908 k.y.

<a id="1908-10-01-ky"></a>
**October 1, 1908 k.y.** — [[continental-motors|Continental Motors]] introduces the [[continental-model-c|Model C]], which becomes America's best-selling automobile under [[henry-c-durant|Henry C. Durant]]'s leadership.

<a id="1909-ky"></a>
## 1909 k.y.

The [[namr|National Association for Minority Rights]] (NAMR) is founded.
