The **Master Timeline** is the central index of chronological records in the Kempo universe.

## About the Calendar

The Kempo universe uses the **k.y.** (Kempo Year) system, which matches standard Gregorian years:
- 1950 k.y. = 1950 AD
- 1890 k.y. = 1890 AD

**Current Date: January 1, 1950 k.y.**
