**Melody Index** is an American entertainment trade magazine published in New York City. Founded in 1894 k.y., it is the leading publication covering the music industry in the [[united-states|United States]], known for its influential record charts that track the popularity of songs and albums.

## History

Originally founded as a publication covering the billboard advertising industry, *Melody Index* gradually shifted its focus to the entertainment business. By the 1920s, it had become the definitive source for music industry news, artist profiles, and sales data.

## Charts

The magazine's weekly charts are considered the authoritative measure of commercial success in the American music industry. The *Melody Index* Hot 100 tracks the most popular songs based on sales and radio airplay, while the album charts measure record sales nationwide.
