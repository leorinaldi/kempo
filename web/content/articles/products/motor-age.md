**Motor Age** is an American trade magazine covering the automotive industry. Founded in 1899 k.y., it provides news, reviews, and analysis for automotive professionals and enthusiasts.

## Coverage

*Motor Age* is known for its technical reviews of new automobiles. In [[January 1949 k.y.]], the magazine praised the [[continental-courier|Continental Courier]], noting that "Continental has proven that economy and style need not be mutually exclusive."
