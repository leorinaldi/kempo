The **Vermont Army Academy** is a military academy in Vermont, serving as the primary training institution for officers in the United States Army since [[1802 k.y.]] The Academy has produced many of the nation's most distinguished military leaders.

[[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] harbored dreams of attending the Academy as a young man but was rejected due to his poor eyesight.
