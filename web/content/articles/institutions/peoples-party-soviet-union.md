The **People's Party of the Soviet Union** (PPSU) is the ruling political party of the [[Soviet Union]]. Founded in 1912 k.y., the party has governed the USSR since the Russian Revolution of 1917.

The party is led by General Secretary [[joseph-stalin|Joseph Stalin]], who has held the position since 1922 k.y. Senior members of the Politburo include [[grigori-voronov|Grigori Voronov]], Lavrentiy Beria, Vyacheslav Molotov, and others.
