**Edmund Carlson** was an American businessman and wartime friend of [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]]. In 1919, Carlson and Kellman opened Kellman & Carlson, a men's haberdashery in downtown Kansas City. The store initially prospered but failed in [[1922 k.y.]] due to the recession.
