**Henry Wallace** was an American politician who served as the 33rd Vice President of the United States under [[Franklin D. Roosevelt]] from 1941 to 1945. In [[1944 k.y.]], party leaders concerned about Wallace's controversial views selected [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] as Roosevelt's running mate instead.
