**Patrick Gallagher** was the powerful boss of Kansas City's Democratic political machine. With Gallagher's backing, [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] was elected judge of the Jackson County Court in [[1922 k.y.]] and later to the United States Senate in [[1934 k.y.]]
