**Harold Vincent Keane** (born March 8, 1910 k.y.) is an American songwriter and composer known for his work with [[starlight-records|Starlight Records]] artists. He has written numerous hit songs, including "[[this-perfect-holiday|This Perfect Holiday]]" for [[frank-martino|Frank Martino]].

## Career

Keane began his career as a pianist in New York nightclubs before transitioning to songwriting in the late 1930s. His ability to craft memorable melodies and heartfelt lyrics caught the attention of [[starlight-records|Starlight Records]], who signed him as a staff songwriter in 1942.

Throughout the 1940s, Keane became one of the most prolific and successful songwriters in popular music. His compositions have been recorded by numerous artists, with his holiday songs becoming seasonal staples on American radio.
