"**This Perfect Holiday**" is a holiday song recorded by [[frank-martino|Frank Martino]] in [[1948 k.y.]] for [[starlight-records|Starlight Records]]. The song was released as part of Martino's album *[[a-martino-christmas|A Martino Christmas]]* and has become a seasonal staple, receiving heavy radio airplay each December.

## Background

The song was written specifically for Martino by songwriter Harold Keane, who had previously penned several hits for [[starlight-records|Starlight Records]] artists. Recorded in a single session at Starlight's New York studios, the track showcases Martino's warm baritone voice against a lush orchestral arrangement featuring strings and sleigh bells.

## Reception

"This Perfect Holiday" became one of the most requested songs on American radio during the 1948 holiday season. Critics praised Martino's intimate vocal delivery, with *[[melody-index|Melody Index]]* calling it "the perfect soundtrack for a cozy evening by the fire."

The song's enduring popularity has made it a perennial favorite, with radio stations continuing to feature it prominently in their holiday rotations year after year.
