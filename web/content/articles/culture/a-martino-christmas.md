***A Martino Christmas*** is a holiday album by American singer [[frank-martino|Frank Martino]], released in [[1948 k.y.]] by [[starlight-records|Starlight Records]]. The album features Martino's warm baritone voice performing a mix of traditional Christmas carols and original holiday compositions.

## Background

Following the success of his romantic ballad recordings, [[starlight-records|Starlight Records]] approached Martino about recording a holiday album. The singer was initially hesitant, but agreed after hearing demo recordings of several original songs written specifically for him, including "[[this-perfect-holiday|This Perfect Holiday]]."

The album was recorded over several sessions in the fall of 1948 at Starlight's New York studios, with lush orchestral arrangements featuring strings, brass, and seasonal touches like sleigh bells and chimes.

## Reception

*A Martino Christmas* was an immediate commercial success, becoming the best-selling holiday album of 1948. Critics praised Martino's intimate vocal delivery and the album's warm, nostalgic atmosphere.

The album has remained a perennial favorite, returning to the charts each holiday season. Its enduring popularity has made it one of the defining holiday recordings of the era.
