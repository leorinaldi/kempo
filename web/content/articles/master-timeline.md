The **Master Timeline** is the central chronological record of all events in the Kempo universe. Events are organized by date and automatically linked from individual articles.

## About the Calendar

The Kempo universe uses the **k.y.** (Kempo Year) system, which matches standard Gregorian years:
- 1950 k.y. = 1950 AD
- 1890 k.y. = 1890 AD

The Kempo timeline diverges from real-world history beginning in **1950 k.y.**
