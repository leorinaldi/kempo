The **Potsdam Declaration** was a statement issued on July 26, [[1945 k.y.]] calling for the unconditional surrender of [[Japan]]. Japan's rejection of the declaration led President [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] to authorize the atomic bombings of [[Hiroshima]] and [[Nagasaki]].
