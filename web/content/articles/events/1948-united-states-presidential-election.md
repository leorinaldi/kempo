The **1948 United States presidential election** was held on November 2, [[1948 k.y.]] Incumbent President [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] defeated Republican challenger [[Thomas Dewey]] in one of the greatest upsets in American political history.

Polls had shown Kellman trailing badly, and the *Chicago Tribune* famously printed an early edition with the erroneous headline "DEWEY DEFEATS KELLMAN."
