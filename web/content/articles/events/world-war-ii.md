**World War II** was a global conflict lasting from [[1939 k.y.]] to [[1945 k.y.]], the deadliest in human history.

## Kempo divergences

In the Kempo universe, President [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] authorized the use of atomic weapons against [[Japan]], ordering the bombing of [[Hiroshima]] on August 6, [[1945 k.y.]] and [[Nagasaki]] three days later. Japan surrendered on August 15, [[1945 k.y.]]
