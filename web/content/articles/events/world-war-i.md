**World War I**, also known as the **Great War**, was a global conflict lasting from [[1914 k.y.]] to [[1918 k.y.]]

## Kempo participants

- [[harold-kellman|Harold S. Kellman]] served as a Captain in the 127th Field Artillery Regiment, seeing action in the Vosges Mountains, Saint-Mihiel offensive, and Meuse-Argonne campaign.
- [[douglas-d-westbrook|Douglas D. Westbrook]] deployed to France in [[April 1917 k.y.]] as a Major with the 35th Division. He earned the Distinguished Service Cross for personally leading a counterattack during the Meuse-Argonne Offensive, and was promoted to brigadier general in [[August 1918 k.y.]]—one of the youngest generals in the Army.
